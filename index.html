International Quality Online Teaching, Inc. or IQON is envisioned to be the "School of Excellence", committed to provide quality English education for international students. We yearn to become globally competitive and responsive to the demands of a technologically advancing society.

Our mission is to become one of the most respected and highly recognized English Language Program providers, adhering to quality programs based on our students' ability and with focus on the effectiveness and success of our graduates to become men and women of competence, compassion and excellence.

Our values are deeply-rooted and made to manifest in all our teachers, instructors, staff and in everything that we do.

We value:

I - Initiative. It is our first step in the series of actions we undertake. Initiative in continuous enrichment, creativity and innovation, collaboration and connectivity in a technologically advancing society. 

Q - Quality. It is defined as fitness for purpose. Our commitment to excellence. 

O - Open-minded. Our open-mindedness propels us for innovation in teaching, creative programs, cultural awareness and diversity. 

N - Noteworthy. All our programs are carried with our student's success in mind.
